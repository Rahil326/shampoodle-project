Project Description

The MGS351 project implements a complete Salesforce CRM solution for Shampoodle, including custom objects for Customers, Dogs, Appointments, Groomers, and Services. The system streamlines appointment scheduling, service tracking, and customer management.

Future enhancements may include automated text/email reminders, online customer booking, mobile-friendly groomer access, and advanced dashboard analytics for business insights. The cloud-based nature of Salesforce allows real-time collaboration and scalable growth for Shampoodle’s operations.
